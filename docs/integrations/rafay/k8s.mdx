The [ngrok Ingress Controller for Kubernetes](https://ngrok.com/blog-post/ngrok-k8s) is the official controller for adding public and secure ingress traffic to your k8s services. This open source Ingress Controller works with any cloud, locally-hosted, or on-premises Kubernetes cluster to provide ingress to your applications, APIs, or other services while also offloading network ingress and middleware execution to ngrok's platform.
